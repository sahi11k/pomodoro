    What is ZenTen?

    ZenTen is a modern adaptation of the Pomodoro Technique, designed to help you achieve your peak focus state - your perfect 10/10. Created by Francesco Cirillo, the underlying time management method breaks work into focused intervals, helping you maintain concentration while preventing mental fatigue. Each focused session is followed by a short break, allowing your mind to recharge. After completing four sessions, you take a longer break to fully refresh. This structured approach helps you maintain sustained productivity throughout your work or study sessions while keeping your mental clarity at its peak. ‚Äî Adapted from Wikipedia

    How to use ZenTen?

    1. Add a task you want to focus on.
    2. Start your focus timer (25 minutes by default).
    3. Work on your task with complete concentration.
    4. When the timer rings, take a short break (5-10 minutes) to reset your mind.
    5. Repeat steps 2-4 until you complete four focus sessions.
    6. After four sessions, take a longer break (15-20 minutes) to fully recharge.
    7. Begin a new cycle when you're ready to achieve your next perfect 10.
